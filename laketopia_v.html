Dates

Friday May 24 thru Monday May 27. Some santa cruz cars may leave friday around noon to get a good spot before dark.

Clinics

Think of a clinic you want to put on. Nothing too serious. In the past we have had naked slacklining, figure drawing, watercolor, nature walks, rock climbing, beach party, and yoga.

Final Location

Kaiser pass road opens on thursday or friday so we can head straight to jackass meadow. It's not a campsite with toilets or anything, just a big field where we probably won't be disturbed my monster trucks. There is usually a little bit of messing around and argueing over where exactly to camp.

CalTopo with some info!

How to get there

Drive up to the east end of huntington lake and take kaiser pass road (east). It's long and windy and narrow. After around 22.5miles, turn left onto a road towards Jackass Meadow Campground. Before the campground, turn left onto Hooper Diversion OHV Route. Or, if you are a modern human, map yourself to 37.2828, -118.9626. Be warned there is no service out there.
